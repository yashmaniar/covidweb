About Corona Virus

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus. We aim to provide awareness regarding this virus and threat caused by it among people. COVID-19 symptoms can be very mild to severe. Some people have no symptoms. The most common signs and symptoms are fever, cough, tiredness, and loss of taste or smell.

There are many steps you can take to reduce your risk of infection from the COVID-19 virus and reduce the risk of spreading it to others. We are here for the awareness of Corona Virus. Morever, we run campaigns that provides food and shelter, beds, oxygen, remdesivir injections, nebulizer for infected patients.
